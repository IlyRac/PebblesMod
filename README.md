<h1>🪨 Pebbles & Twigs</h1>

<p><strong>Breathe life into your world by adding stackable pebbles and tree twigs that transform empty terrain into a detailed and lively environment. From forest floors littered with wood debris to rocky cave floors and vast ocean beds, this mod enhances the vanilla atmosphere with small, immersive details that make every biome feel more organic and unique.</strong></p>

<hr>

<details>
  <summary>✨ Features</summary>

<h3>🪨 The Pebble System</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>10 Stone Variants:</strong> Includes Andesite, Diorite, Granite, Stone, Deepslate, Tuff, Sandstone, Red Sandstone, Blackstone, and End Stone.</li>
    <li><strong>Stackable Density:</strong> You can stack up to <strong>4 pebbles</strong> in a single block space to create varied clusters and unique terrain details.</li>
    <li><strong>Interactive Projectiles:</strong> Every pebble can be thrown to deal damage or fired from <strong>Dispensers</strong> to create automated rock shooters.</li>
  </ul>

<h3>🪵 The Twig System</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>11 Wood Variants:</strong> Supports all vanilla types including Oak, Spruce, Birch, Jungle, Acacia, Dark Oak, Mangrove, Cherry, Pale Oak, and Nether variants.</li>
    <li><strong>Dynamic Placement:</strong> Place on top of blocks for a <strong>"Standing"</strong> look or on sides for a <strong>"Sleeping"</strong> flat look.</li>
    <li><strong>Axe Interaction:</strong> Use an axe on any placed twig to <strong>strip it</strong>, creating a clean "Stripped Twig" variant.</li>
  </ul>

<h3>🌍 World & Mechanics</h3>
  <ul> 
    <li><strong>Natural Biome Spawning:</strong> Blocks generate logically throughout your world. Find Sandstone pebbles in Deserts, warped twigs in the Nether, and End Stone pebbles across the End islands.</li>
    <li><strong>Vibrant Environments:</strong> Twigs populate forest floors and lush biomes, while pebbles appear on the surface, inside cave systems, and across <strong>ocean floors and riverbeds</strong>.</li>
    <li><strong>Full Compatibility:</strong> All blocks are <strong>Waterloggable</strong> for underwater detailing and twigs are <strong>Flammable</strong> for realistic environmental interaction.</li>
  </ul>

<h3>🛠️ Crafting & Utility</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Full Survival Integration:</strong> Every item is craftable! Pebbles can be crafted from their respective stone blocks and <strong>re-crafted back into full blocks</strong>.</li>
    <li><strong>Wood Processing:</strong> Twigs can be crafted using Logs, Wood, and Stripped variants.</li>
    <li><strong>Creative Ready:</strong> All items are neatly sorted into the <strong>Natural</strong> and <strong>Building</strong> Creative Mode tabs for easy access.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>🎮 How to Use</summary>
  <ol>
    <li><strong>Stacking:</strong> Right-click an existing pebble with another of the same type to increase the cluster size (up to 4).</li>
    <li><strong>Orientation:</strong> Aim at the top of a block for vertical twigs; aim at the side or bottom of a block for horizontal twigs.</li>
    <li><strong>Precision Placement:</strong> Horizontal twigs follow trapdoor logic! Aim at the <strong>top half</strong> of a block's side to place the twig at the top of the block space, or the <strong>bottom half</strong> to place it on the surface.</li>
    <li><strong>Stripping:</strong> Use any Axe on a placed twig to strip it.</li>
    <li><strong>Defense:</strong> Right-click with a pebble to throw it, or use a <strong>Dispenser</strong> for base defense.</li>
  </ol>
</details>
